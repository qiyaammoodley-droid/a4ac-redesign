# A4AC Architects Website Redesign

### Company Chosen

**Company Name:** A4AC Architects

**Industry:** Architecture, Urban Design and Interior Design

**Current Website:** https://www.a4ac.co.za/

---

### How I Found the Company

I found A4AC Architects through a Google search while looking for South African companies with websites that could benefit from a modern redesign. Their work and portfolio immediately stood out, making them a great candidate for this project.

---

### Why I Chose This Company

I chose A4AC Architects because they have an impressive portfolio of residential, commercial and industrial projects that deserves to be presented through a more modern and user-friendly website. Their existing website contains valuable information and showcases high-quality projects, making it an excellent foundation for a redesign while maintaining the company's professional identity.

---

### Why the Website Needs a Redesign

Although the current website contains useful information, there are several areas where the overall user experience could be improved.

Some of the issues I identified include:

- The website has an outdated visual design compared to modern architecture websites.
- The layout feels crowded in several sections, making information harder to scan.
- Navigation could be simplified to help users reach important pages more quickly.
- Calls-to-action are limited, making it less obvious how users can contact the company or explore projects.
- The overall branding lacks consistency across different pages.
- The website could better showcase completed projects through larger imagery and a cleaner gallery layout.
- Mobile responsiveness could be improved to provide a better experience across different screen sizes.

These observations formed the basis for the redesign while preserving the company's professional identity and architectural branding.

---

## 3.2 The Redesign

The redesigned website was developed using **React** with **functional components** and **React Router** for navigation. **Tailwind CSS** was used to create a clean, responsive and modern user interface.

The redesign maintains A4AC Architects' identity while improving usability and presentation through:

- Responsive layouts for desktop, tablet and mobile devices.
- A modern homepage with a strong hero section.
- Clear navigation between all pages.
- Dedicated About, Projects, Services and Contact pages.
- Reusable React components for improved maintainability.
- A modern project gallery with interactive image viewing.
- An embedded Google Map and contact section to improve accessibility.
- Consistent typography, spacing and visual hierarchy throughout the website.

The goal of the redesign was not to change the company's identity, but to present its services and portfolio through a more modern, accessible and engaging user experience.
